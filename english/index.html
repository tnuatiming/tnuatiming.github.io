Tnua Timing operates under Tnua Computing and Navigation Systems Inc. and its expertise is in measuring, mainly, motor sports events and other. Operations began in 2009 providing timing services to Israel Enduro league and expanded to other branches, including:

  • Baja League Israel - Laps Desert Racing (Ze'elim, Ashalim, Yeruham, etc.)
  • Screening Formula Israel in 2012 - private cars on an asphalt surface in the form of Gymkhana
  • Rally Cross Country League - Off-Road Special tests (Yemen field)
  • Rally Sprint League - a short special tests cars in Rally (Katzrin, Arad)
  • ALL MOUNTAIN League - bike downhill racing
  • MotoCross League
  • SuperMoto League
  • SuperBike League
  • Karting League

The company also offers its services to road tests for cars and motorcycles for various media and for organizations who have training and driving experiences, in seminars or fun days for employees.
